What to Expect

At Chantrieri, our design philosophy centers on creating tablescapes that feel personal, expressive, and thoughtfully layered. Everything begins with the questions you answer about your event—your mood, vision, and atmosphere.

From those details, we identify the vintage pieces, fine China, crystal glassware, kerosene lamps, Italian art glass, Victorian accents, and so much more from our wide range of vintage and antique inventory that best fit your gathering.

You can then choose to use these items yourself or have us curate one or two complete tablescape concepts. Every design reflects your story with character, charm, intention, and a style that may be classic, elegant, eclectic, or uniquely your own.

Some of the services we provide include: Curated tablescapes for all types of gatherings—ranging from one-on-one intimate date nights to bridal engagements, weddings, celebrations, and meaningful moments of all kinds. Our table settings are designed to be used in many different ways and across a wide range of styles. From high noon teas and photo shoots to cozy brunches, intimate evenings for two, or elegant affairs, each setting is thoughtfully created using our creative knowledge, experience, and carefully curated inventory. Our Vision

Storied, Substantial, Quietly Beautiful

At Chantrieri, our vision is to create one-of-a-kind tablescapes shaped entirely around each client's event—chosen colors, themes, atmosphere. Guided by our professional ornamentalist, we transform these inspirations into thoughtful, artful designs using our curated vintage, antique, and specialty pieces.

Our vision is to bring forward classic, nostalgic, and eclectic tableware paired with extraordinary conversation pieces—rare, storied objects that captivate guests, spark curiosity, and invite them to admire the history behind each detail.

Signature Experience

The Uranium Glow Soirée

Designed for gatherings of up to fifteen guests, the Uranium Glow Soirée is an immersive tablescape experience where glass and light take center stage.

Featuring a curated mix of Depression glass, uranium, cadmium, and other distinctive pieces, each setting is brought to life through thoughtfully provided lighting.

Tea Party Setting Options

Our Tea Party settings are designed to move effortlessly between styles, moods, and moments.

From polished and traditional to imaginative and expressive, each table is layered with intention using pieces from our curated vintage inventory.

Options range from proper Victorian and classic English tea, to Victorian Gothic, storybook whimsy, and Mad Hatter–inspired playfulness.

We also offer softer, celebratory styles perfect for girls’ nights, Sweet 16s, and princess-themed gatherings, as well as intimate, understated settings ideal for smaller, refined occasions.

Every tea party setting is thoughtfully built to reflect the tone you’re drawn to—elegant or unexpected, structured or spontaneous—allowing each table to feel personal, layered, and visually memorable.
